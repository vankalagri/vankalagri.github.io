About Vankal Agri Solution

Vankal Agri Solution is a pioneering agri-biotechnology company that is committed to making a positive impact in the agricultural industry. Founded in 2021, the company has rapidly grown to become a Limited company, thanks to its team of highly skilled technocrats who are passionate about developing environmentally friendly products. Vankal Agri Solution is dedicated to keeping up with the radical changes in agriculture and is constantly striving to develop innovative and sustainable solutions for farmers. With an unwavering focus on quality, the company has established itself as a leader in the industry, and its products are highly sought after. Whether you are a farmer or a distributor, Vankal Agri Solution is the go-to company for all your agricultural needs.

Our Products

At Vankal Agri Solution, we take pride in offering a broad range of organic fertilizers that provide farmers with an effective and environmentally conscious alternative to traditional fertilizers.

Trichoderma viride

We offer Trichoderma viride, a potent biofungicide derived from fungus that effectively suppresses fungal diseases in soil and seeds. Our product is a popular choice among farmers looking for sustainable and natural crop health solutions.

Arbuscular Mycorrhiza

This fungi facilitate host plants to grow vigorously under stressful conditions by mediating a series of complex communication events between the plant and the fungus leading to enhanced photosynthetic rate and other gas exchange-related traits as well as increased water uptake.
